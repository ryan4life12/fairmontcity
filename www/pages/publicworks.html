Public Works Department

MISSION STATEMENT

It is the mission of the Fairmont City Public Works Department to achieve the highest possible quality of life for our residents and visitors through effective planning, asset management, operation and maintenance of the Village infrastructure by the most efficient means.

DEPARTMENT FUNCTIONS

Overall operation, maintenance and long term improvement programs for the public infrastructure, consisting of sanitary sewer collection system, sanitary sewage pump stations, storm water collection system, street repairs and maintenance, and Village parks and buildings.

The Public Works Department is also responsible for collection and disposal of municipal solid waste collected from homes, and periodic large item pickup.
