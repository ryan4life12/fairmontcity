Welcome to the "City of Good Neighbors"

Welcome

Welcome to the Village of Fairmont City. We are the city of good neighbors; we strive to create an outstanding way of life for our community. This is done through providing a well-protected community for our citizens to thrive in a place of culture, history, and future expansion.
