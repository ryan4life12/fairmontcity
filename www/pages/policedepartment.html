Police Department

The Fairmont City Police Department takes pride in serving and protecting the community. The department works to improve the everyday life for all residents of the village by reducing the problems associated with crime through: crime suppression and prevention while jointly educating its officers and the public, developing a pro-active partnership with the community and in accordance with constitutional rights to enforce the laws and preserve the peace.
