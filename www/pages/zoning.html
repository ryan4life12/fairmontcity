The Fairmont City Zoning Board of Appeals

The Fairmont City Zoning Board of Appeals works to insure the orderly and compatible development of land use throughout the Village, and to enforce the Village Zoning Ordinance # 601, adopted on April 02, 2003.

The Board Consists of seven members, appointed to four year staggered terms, by the Mayor and Trustees. The current members are:
